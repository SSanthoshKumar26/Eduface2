Moving on to our next point. What is a Product? A product is a tangible or intangible item that is created once and sold to many customers with little or no customization. Examples: Mobile apps (Instagram, WhatsApp) Software products (Windows, MS Office) Physical items (laptops, smartphones) Key Characteristics: Same product for all users Scalable (one product → many customers) Development cost is mostly upfront Revenue grows as users increase ... Moving on to our next point. What is a Service? A service is an activity or solution provided to a customer, often customized based on their requirements. Examples: Software development services IT consulting Cloud deployment support Maintenance and technical support Key Characteristics: Tailored to customer needs Continuous interaction with clients Depends heavily on skilled manpower Revenue is project-based or time-based ...
