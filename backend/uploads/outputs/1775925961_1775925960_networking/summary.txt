Networks facilitate the sharing and communication of resources among devices through various configurations, including local area networks (LAN), wide area networks (WAN), wireless networks (WLAN), and virtual private networks (VPN). These networks can be categorized into bus, star, ring, and mesh topologies, each with its own advantages and disadvantages. Networks also involve the use of routers, switches, hubs, and modems to connect devices and facilitate data transmission over the internet.
