Networks are essential for connecting devices and sharing resources, with various types including Local Area Networks (LANS), Wide Area Networks (WANs), Wireless Networks (WNAs), Virtual Private Networks (VPNs), and more. These networks can be categorized into different topologies such as Bus, Star, Ring, and Mesh configurations, each suited for specific applications. Additionally, devices like routers, switches, hubs, and modems play crucial roles in facilitating communication across these networks.
